## 1. Company Overview

### Founding & Mission
Anthropic is an American artificial intelligence (AI) company headquartered in San Francisco founded in 2021 by former members of OpenAI, including siblings Daniela Amodei and Dario Amodei. The co-founders structured Anthropic as a public benefit corporation, in which the board can legally prioritize its mission of ensuring that "transformative AI helps people and society flourish."

The company was founded due to concerns over a lack of commitment to safety at OpenAI, recognizing the potential power AI held and growing increasingly concerned about potential dangers including misinformation, bias, and unintended harmful behavior.

### Funding & Growth
As of February 2026, Anthropic has an estimated value of $380 billion. The company has secured significant investments, including:
- In September 2025, Anthropic completed a Series F funding round, raising $13 billion at a post-money valuation of $183 billion
- Google agreed in January 2025 to invest $1B+ in Anthropic, building on its past investments of $2B
- Its latest funding round was a Series G round on Feb 12, 2026 for $30B

Anthropic has 4,074 employees as of Jan 26.

### Company Size & Structure
Anthropic operates as a research-driven organization with internal governance structures designed to maintain its safety-first mission. Anthropic maintains a hybrid governance structure, with an internal body called the Long-Term Benefit Trust, tasked with overseeing the company's public mission. This Trust has the power to appoint independent board members and guide corporate decisions toward safety and ethics, even against market pressures.

## 2. Product & Technology

### What They Build
Anthropic has developed a family of large language models (LLMs) named Claude. Claude is Anthropic's flagship AI chatbot, designed to be more reliable and steerable than other large language models.

### Key Features & Functionality
Claude uses Constitutional AI, a framework that allows the AI to adhere to predefined ethical guidelines. Claude features built-in safeguards to help minimize misinformation and biased responses in addition to allowing users to adjust Claude's behavior to fit specific needs.

The Claude family includes multiple models:
- Claude 3 Opus: Performs exceptionally well in Undergraduate Level Knowledge (86.8%), Grade School Math (95.0%), Multilingual Math (90.7%), and Code (84.9%)
- Sonnet strikes a balance between performance and efficiency, making it great for general use without being too resource-heavy
- Haiku is the smallest and most efficient, ideal for simpler tasks where speed and resource savings are key

### Technical Architecture
At the core of Anthropic's work and central to its goal of safe and ethical AI, Constitutional AI represents a framework that AI must refer to and implement whenever responding to user inquiries.

Anthropic maintains a strong research focus on interpretability and alignment, with ongoing work in mechanistic interpretability and advanced safety techniques.

### Competitive Positioning
Unlike other chatbots, Claude uses Constitutional AI, a framework that allows the AI to adhere to predefined ethical guidelines. While other companies rush to put out AI products as fast as they can, Anthropic chooses not to release models above certain capability thresholds until it can develop sufficiently robust safety measures.
