# Application Templates for YC Companies

## Cold Outreach to Founder/CTO

**Subject:** ML Engineer - [Your Name] - [Company Name] AI/ML role

Hi [Name],

I've been following [Company Name] since YC [Batch] and was excited to see your ML Engineer opening. Your approach to [specific aspect of product] is exactly the kind of problem I'm passionate about.

Brief background: [2–3 relevant credentials]. I was particularly impressed by [specific blog/launch/interview]. It seems you're tackling [technical challenge], which aligns with my experience in [your expertise].

I'd love to learn more about the technical challenges your team is facing. Would you be open to a 15-minute chat this week?

Best,  
[Your Name]  
[LinkedIn] [GitHub]

## Follow-up (1–2 weeks after applying)

**Subject:** Following up - ML Engineer application - [Your Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm following up on my application for the ML Engineer role at [Company Name]. I'm still very interested. Since applying, I've been thinking about [specific idea or insight]. [One sentence insight.]

I'd appreciate any update on my application or a quick chat on how I could contribute.

Best,  
[Your Name]

## Cover Letter (ML Engineer) – Key Sections

- **Opening:** Interest in [Company Name], reference YC batch and mission.
- **Why them:** Specific product aspect + how it connects to your experience.
- **Technical fit:** ML experience (years, domain, one concrete outcome); MLOps/infra; leadership if relevant.
- **Why this company:** Product vision, technical challenges, team/culture (one sentence each).
- **What you'd contribute:** 2–3 concrete contributions aligned to their stack/challenges.
- **Close:** Thank them, offer to discuss.

**Customization:** Reference a recent post, launch, or technical challenge; use terms from the job description; keep under one page.

## LinkedIn

- **Connection request:** "Hi [Name], I'm an ML Engineer interested in [Company Name]'s work on [area]. Would love to connect and learn more."
- **After connecting:** Short note that you're interested in their ML role and would value a brief chat on team and technical challenges.

## Notes

1. Be specific: mention blog posts, features, or founder interviews.
2. Show research: tech stack, challenges, market.
3. Quantify where possible (e.g. improved X by Y%).
4. Match language from the job posting.
5. Keep emails and messages brief; proofread.
